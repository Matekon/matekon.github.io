Matekon's home

    Hi, I am Vincent Laberge, aka Matekon, a master in mathematics and an amateur composer from Montréal.

    As a thinker, I enjoy finding new ways of seeing familiar topics, mainly in mathematics and philosophy. I therefore want to share with you my take on them.

    As an artist, I consider myself pretty creative, but I am also convinced that creativity is mainly a derivative process and that it is impossible to come up with a completely original idea. I am inspired by many composers, mainly modern, and I want to share with you my take on their musical findings.

    You can read here about my music taste.
